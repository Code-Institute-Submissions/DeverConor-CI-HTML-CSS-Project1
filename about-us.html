Our Materials

When producing minimal furniture, the material selection is just as important as the design of the piece itself. We hand select each board from sustainable sources and mills that process only the highest quality material, considering colour selection, grain pattern, and board width. We use North American timber to reduce our environmental footprint and live out our sustainability convictions. Solid wood is a natural product - it varies in colour and grain depending on the origin of the tree. This means that you will have colour variations, patterns, maybe even knots in your piece, and we hope you join us in celebrating the uniqueness of this incredible natural material.

Our Philosophy

Honesty is important to us in both our furniture and our business. We make exceptional furniture devoid of drama or ego. Our designs are simple on purpose to ground your space without demanding the spotlight. We conduct our business with honesty, openness, and a desire to build a community of like-minded people working towards a common goal.
