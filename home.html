Ethos

The modern world is a world of speed, convenience, and efficiency. However in a rush to modernise and reduce costs some furniture makers have lost sight of what is truly important. Goldfinch Designs makes furniture using the finest locally sourced materials, constructed by the hands of craftsman. Our focus is not so save money or make something that looks good. Our goal is to make bespoke pieces that last for years, providing the most beautiful and well made furniture that you can love for years.

Materials

The logging industry has been at the forefront of the Canadian economy and culture for decades, and for good reason. Carved out of a temperate rainforest Vancouver is surrounded by the most beautiful and long lasting lumber all over the globe. Whether it is Oak, Walnut, or Ash, we will build it and finish it to your exact requirements.

Construction

Combining master craftsmanship and simple elegant design we endeavour to make pieces that are timeless. We believe it is better to invest in pieces that we love once and keep them for a long time rather than getting something cheaper everytime you move. Its better on the pocket and on the planet.
